How to play the game of set:

Several games can be played with these cards, all involving the concept of a set. A set consists of three cards satisfying all of these conditions:

    They all have the same number or have three different numbers. (1,2,3)

    • They all have the same symbol or have three different symbols. (triangle,circle,square)

    • They all have the same shading or have three different shadings. (filled,blank,striped)

    They all have the same color or have three different colors. (blue,green,red)
